How to Build a Hardware Product

aka, why hardware is hard!

Building a hardware product is not easy, but after the on-time launch of Leeo, I felt like I should share with you how we did it. The following chart goes through the various phases of IoT product development (Prototyping, EVT, DVT, PVT, and finally Mass Production). It shows what needs doing, and more importantly who needs to talk to whom, to get everything done in time.
As the project manager on the product, I was responsible for setting the deadlines for each gate and milestones and getting the right people moving in the right direction. Please let me know if you have any feedback about the chart, I'd love to chat about it!
Good luck!
